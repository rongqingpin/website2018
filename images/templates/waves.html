Given a group of propagating waves, how to obtain their speed & source location?



Automatical tracking & modeling provides the speed & source location estimation


Motivation

Given a group of propagating waves (moving objects), how to keep track of their location & deformation automatically?

View details »

Workflow & Techniques

  1. Object Detection: identify the waves in a given 2D image
  2. Object Tracking: track the wave location between successive images
  3. Modeling: construct simplified model of the propagating waves to assist in further analysis

View details »

Outcome

Estimated the wave features based on the simplified model:

  • propagation speed and direction
  • source location
