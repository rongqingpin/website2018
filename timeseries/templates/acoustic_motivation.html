Acoustic Event Detection

Extract acoustic source from time series; identify and visualize event-associated structures

Motivation: Reduction of Aircraft Noise


  • Sustained exposure to aircraft noise (e.g., air crews) can lead to hearing damage and loss.
  • Expansion of airports threatens more residences with noise pollution.

My research focused on the noise from air flow, since over 50% of aircraft noise comes from the engine exhaust.


Problem Statement



Given:

  • Aircraft noise measured as acoustic signals (time series)
  • Flow quantities (e.g. velocity) measured as 1D time series / 2D images, which contain both
    • Noise-producing structures (events)
    • Those unrelated to noise production

Task:

  • Isolate the noise-producing events
  • Identify & the event-associated structures

Approach:

  • Event detection algorithm (time-frequency signal processing, statistical signal processing, pattern recognition)
  • New time-frequency & statistical visualization techniques for structure identification
