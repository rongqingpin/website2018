Motivation & Problem Statement

Given the acoustic signals (e.g. the noise produced by airplanes), can we identify the source (e.g. certain flow structures of the engine exhaust) which produce them?

View details »

Workflow & Techniques

  1. Data Cleaning: denoising; detrending; down-sampling; faulty data detection & replacement
  2. Data Exploration: statistical signal processing; spectral analysis
  3. Big Data Problem: resolved through constructing representative signals
  4. Event Detection Algorithm: statistical signal processing; time-frequency signal processing; pattern recognition; criterion optimization
  5. Algorithm Testing & Evaluation: randomized signals; synthetic signals; real data
  6. Event-Based Analysis: statistics; signal filtering; conditional averaging; event-conditioned analysis

Learn More »

Outcome

  • Identified the location, time & frequency of occurrence of the acoustic sources of high speed jet flows (over 90% accuracy)
  • Extracted the structure features and the flow activities associated with noise production
