Input:

Two-dimensional flow without apparent structures (other than the shock structures and the shear layers)



Output:

Structure evolution and pathways obtained from new visualization techniques, which combine statistical and time-frequency techniques


Motivation

  • Give a flow field (2D images), how to extract the hidden structures and visualize their evolution and pathways?
  • Can we make the conventional signal analysis techniques more intuitive?

View details »

Workflow & Techniques

  1. Data Cleaning: denoising; detrending; down-sampling; faulty data detection & replacement
  2. Data Exploration: statistical signal processing; spectral analysis; time-frequency signal processing
  3. Big Data Problem: resolved through constructing Hot-Spots
  4. New Visualization Techniques:
    • Peak Frequency Distribution
    • Normalized Spectrum Distribution
    • Peak Correlation Distribution
    • Space-Time Correlation
    • Frequency-Resolved Space-Time Correlation
